My Process

  1. Inquiry & scope: We discuss what you need and agree on timeline and deliverables.
  2. Sample/readthrough: I do an initial read and create high-level notes.
  3. Draft deliverable: I return annotated documents and a summary of actionable items.
  4. Revision/Q&A: You review and we clarify anything necessary.
